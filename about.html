About E-Section Marcher


The E-Section Marcher System replaces the current paper accountability version of section marcher slips at VMI.

Our product will allow:

  • A simple online interface to take accountability
  • Automated process of submitting accountability slips
  • Improved speed of the process for Corps academic accountability tenfold.
  • Alleviates manual labor and stress for personnel involved
  • Reduced waiting time from submission by the cadet to the time it is processed by the Commandant's Staff

E-Section Marcher Accessibility

The following groups are eligible to use E-Section Marcher. All users must use their assigned @vmi.edu email address.
  • Cadets
  • Professors
  • Department Secretary
  • Commandant's Staff

Description

As a group Team 6 will create the E-section marcher product during our CIS-490 class. This product will be allowed online so that cadet within VMI will have the ability to take role online. We have created has four main groups: cadet, secretary, commandant staff, and professor. Each group has its same functionality as compared to paper section marcher process. For the cadet side, they will be able to see exactly what classes they are section marchers in, See your history of section marchers submitted, Instructions page, Attendance and Your cadet info. The Secretary side of things will be able to view the submitted attendances per day, search by words and find cadets or courses. Commandants staff will be able to view the full report of submitted attendances per day, search by letters typed in and find cadets or courses. The professor side of things will be able to take roll if the top three section marchers aren't there. As a group we have got our product reviewed by multiple different professional and peer-related groups. Each user has an instructions page that explains how to use the system and is there for VMI's sake in making sure that tolerance is not an excuse.
