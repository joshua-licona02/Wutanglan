About E-Section Marcher


The E-Section Marcher System replaces the current paper accountability version of section marcher slips at VMI.

Our product will allow Cadets to:

  • Use a simple online interface to take accountability
  • Automate the process of sending the accountability slips
  • Improve the speed of the process for Corps academic accountability tenfold.
  • Alleviates manual labor and stress for personnel involved.
  • Allow for a reduced waiting time from the time of submission by the cadet to the time it is processed by the Commandant's Staff
